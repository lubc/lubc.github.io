# About

Hi, I'm Luciano, Senior Software Engineer from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I work at OmbuLabs, a remote company focused on building web applications. I'm also coach and organizer at Rails Girls, an OmbuLabs sponsored event to introduce women to programming.

I studied Computer science for a few of years at UNLaM, but nowadays I continue my education by reading books and taking online courses. I also like to frequently assist tech events, programming conferences and meetups.

I often write articles for the OmbuLabs Blog and FastRuby Blog, and I colaborate on open source projects.

Programming is my real passion, but not the only one. I also love traveling, practicing calisthenics and photography/filmmaking.
